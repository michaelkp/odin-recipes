Corn Salad

A quick and delicious side for any meal!

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Frsh Cilantro
  • Green Onion
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Lime
  • Salt and PepperS

Steps

  1. Mix corn kernels, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, onion, cilantro, and green onion together in a large bowl. Add a drizzle of olive oil. Squeeze lime juice into the bowl and season with salt and pepper.
